INSTRUCTIONS !

·  The cut-off mark for the Entrance examination is 40%.

· Candidates who scored up to 40% and above are successful and are hereby invited for oral examination/screening

·  Successful candidates are expected to pay the sum of five thousand Naira (N5000 Non-refundable) into the college’s Bank account for the oral Examination/screening.

ACCOUNT NAME: ST. CHARLES BORROMEO COLLEGE OF NURSING, ONITSHA

ACCOUNT NUMBER: 1014412009
BANK NAME: ZENITH BANK

Come to the College with your teller and collect your receipt as your evidence of payment.

You may come to the College with your Bank (ATM) Card and pay via our POS Device.

The Interview is STRICTLY for those who scored 40% and above.

Kindly adjust to the NEW DATE BELOW assigned to you for the interview.

First Day: Wednesday 30th August, 2023
Batch A: Exam Nos 1 – 470

Second Day: Thursday 31st August, 2023

Batch B: Exam Nos 471 – 793

Third Day: Friday 1st Sept., 2023

Batch C: Exam Nos 794 – 1030

Time: 8am
Venue: College of Nursing Sciences, St Charles Borromeo Hospital, Onitsha

REQUIREMENTS: Bring along the following:

1. Your Entrance Exam Photocard (IF YOU ARE NOT WITH YOUR PHOTOCARD, NO INTERVIEW FOR YOU)
2. Original & photocopies of your WAEC/NECO/NABTEB certificates, Secondary School Testimonial, Birth Certificate/Baptismal Card/Sworn Affidavit and Marriage Certificate (if married).
