Admission Now Open !! For the Year 2023. REGISTER NOW

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS:

  1. 5 Credits in WASSCE, NECO, GCE, in
    not more than2 sittings to include: English Onitsha.
    language, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and Biology.
  2. Photocopies of Credentials, Birth Certificate/Sworn Age Declaration,
    Marriage Certificate (if married) and
    Baptismal Card
  3. 3 recent passport photographs.
  4. Photocopy of school testimonial.
 
 

HOW TO APPLY:

  1. Application forms are obtainable from College on
    payment of non- refundable fee of Fifteen Thousand
    Naira only (N15,000]
  2. Pay into into any Zenith Bank, Account Name is  St.Charles Borromeo College of  Nursing.  Account Number: 1014412009
  3. Come with E-Payment and Teller

 

NB: The Entrance examination is
computer based (CBT] not written

DATE & VENUE FOR EXAMINATION

  • Date
  • 22nd July, 2023.
  • Venue
  • To be communicated to you later
  • Time:
  • 8am prompt

For more Enquiries Contact: +2349070497013 +2348090751590 +2349083391206
