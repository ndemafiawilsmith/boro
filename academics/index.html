What sets us apart?

Personalized attention

Every student is given personalized training putting into consideration; differences in their point of view, characters or relationships, rather than considering the facts in a general or abstract way.

Smart Classes

This includes; use of projectors during lectures and availability of computers for research purposes.

Student Clubs

From The Provost's Desk

Significant advances in biomedical science and the complexity of health care have necessitated the need for a new crop of professional clinical nurses who would bring in their critical thinking skills to effectively coordinate, manage and evaluate care for a group of patients in complex health systems. To ensure this, the institution has put in place a sound curriculum, highly qualified lecturers and instructors, adequate modern and state of the art facilities and equipment, a conducive environment for teaching, learning, clinical practice and research, above all the utilization of evidence-based practice by the graduates of this programme. The College highly qualified educators bring with them contemporary knowledge and pedagogical insights from diverse backgrounds. All our programmes take due cognizance of the 3 domains of educational objective (cognitive, affective and psychomotor, which must seek to build the desirable competence of graduates of the College of Nursing Sciences that will render care that is in tune with international best practices. College of Nursing Sciences, St Charles Borromeo Specialist Hospital; the pacesetter in nursing education and fully accredited by the Nursing and Midwifery Council of Nigeria is the place to be. We urge you to schedule a visit for a personal experience, we guarantee you will be amazed by our journey so far.  May God bless us all

Mrs. Theresa Aniedu

Provost. (AG)
